The questions every team asks.

Honest answers, up front.

Does this live in our app, under our brand?

Yes. BlinkUp drops into your app and website — fans see your feature name and your sponsors, never ours. We set up locations, offers, and partners; you're live in weeks, with ongoing support and reporting.

We already run a loyalty program. Does this replace it?

No — it feeds it. Check-ins flow into the rewards platform you already run as points, milestones, and streaks. Attendance becomes a new earning behavior, layered onto what you have. No rip-and-replace.

Most of our fans have location services turned off.

Arena and bar check-ins use a simple geofence tap-in. Home watch parties verify over Bluetooth — no location permission required (patent-pending).

Who recruits and manages the bars?

We do. BlinkUp builds and onboards your watch-party bar network for you — outreach, deal setup, and game-day support. Bars join free and set their own deals.

What stops people from gaming the rewards?

Verified presence plus one-time redemption links. Every deal and prize is claimed once, tied to an identified fan.
